ABOUT US

Leading Gaming Service Provider

AquixStudios is an Italian-registered company operating globally, known as one of the largest and most reputable digital service providers in the gaming market, bringing thousands of businesses to life since 2019!

We offer a wide range of services, including Graphic Design, and 3D Modelling, with a strong specialization in Minecraft-related services such as Game Design, Community events planning, Maps Building, and Server Plugin Development & Configuration!

We have completed over 5000 projects and satisfied more than 3000 unique clients, including notable figures and E-sports teams!
